    We are a restaurant chain that specializes in burgers, egg burgers, and egg sandwiches. We originated from Nanjing, the capital city of Jiangsu province in China, and also have two locations in Shanghai as well. Eggsoul is known for our tall American-style burgers with eggs, as well as our fast-casual dining environment. If you are looking for a place to enjoy some hearty and delicious food in Nanjing, you might want to check out Eggsoul.
